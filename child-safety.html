Child Safety Standards (CSAE)

Valyd is committed to protecting minors and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We take safety seriously and enforce strict rules to keep our platform positive, respectful, and safe.

Prohibited Content

Valyd strictly prohibits:

  • Any content involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexualization of minors in any form
  • Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
  • Attempts to share, request, or distribute sexual content involving minors

Reporting and Enforcement

Users can report accounts or content directly within the Valyd app. Reports are reviewed promptly and may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent bans.

Legal Compliance

When we detect or receive reports of potential CSAE or CSAM, we take immediate action and comply with all applicable laws. Where required, we report relevant information to appropriate regional or national authorities.
